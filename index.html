About me

Hi! My name is Diego Ramon. I am a third year student at the HvA (Hogeschool van Amsterdam)

I am following the 'Communication & Multimedia Design' course at the HVA and have certainly found my place here! To this day I still have a lot of fun and motivation for it and I definitely plan to make this my profession later.

Before I joined this course, I followed a 'Game Development' course at Media College in Amsterdam, but I soon discovered that creating and designing websites was more of a profession for me. However, this did not stop me from completing the course, which actually gave me a significant head start at my current course!

Besides creating websites I have lots of hobbies, such as working out, cooking, taking photographs, gaming, and going out with my girlfriend!
